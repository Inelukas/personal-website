About me

Hi, I'm Lukas from Germany. Well, kind of. I've spent the last 8 years wandering through East Asia, gradually shedding my German identity. Now, I'm back in my home country, trying to piece together the remnants of my former self. In the midst of this personal reconstruction, I've decided to dive into programming. Why? Because the cold, hard truth is that my university degree won't land me a job. And there's no way I'm going back to my old life as a disillusioned journalist. Sure, I spent the last 2-3 years in that world, but I was never truly good at it and never really learned it. So here I am, navigating a new path and hoping to find my place again.
